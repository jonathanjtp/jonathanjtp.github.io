Join the BUILD Community

THE BUILDS isnt only a website it's also a community of car enthusiasts helping each other build better cars.

Why Share Your Build?

  • Document your journey for your own records
  • Help others learn from your successes and mistakes
  • Get advice and feedback from experienced enthusiasts
  • Connect with people who have the same car
  • Show off your hard work to people who appreciate it

Community Guidelines

  1. Be respectful no personal attacks or gatekeeping
  2. Share real experiences honesty helps everyone learn
  3. Give credit where due mention shops, tuners, and helpers
  4. Include details part numbers, prices, and results help others
  5. Update your Builds, people love following active builds

Submit Your Build Thread

Ready to share your build? We'd love to see your project.

What We're Looking For

  • Detailed documentation of your build process
  • High quality photos showing the car and modifications
  • Complete parts lists with brands and part numbers
  • Honest assessment of what worked and what didn't
  • Your personal story and why you built the car

How to Submit

  1. Gather your photos (minimum 5, we recommend 10-15)
  2. Write up your build story and modification list
  3. Email everything to Jpaniagua@student.clayton.edu
  4. Include your location and social media handles
  5. We'll review and reach out within 24 hours
