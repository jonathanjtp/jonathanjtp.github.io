Our Story

The Builds is a place for car enthusiasts to share what their builds, keep track of their progress, and connect with other enthusiast who also have a story about building cars. Its a place where a community can share real builds, real experiences, and connections you only get from actually being involved with cars.

Our Mission

My mission is to make a place where anyone beginners to experienced builders can showcase their projects, learn from others, and get inspired. I believe every build has a story.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Detailed build threads from real car enthusiasts
  • Progress updates and modification breakdowns
  • Advice, tips, and shared experiences
  • A community of Car Enthusiast

About the Creator

This website was created by Jonathan ,a car enthusiast who has also built and modified his own projects. From daily drivers to track builds, the goal is to create a place where people can learn, connect, and showcase their work.
